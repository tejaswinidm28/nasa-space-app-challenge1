Our Mission

The PACE Education Project is dedicated to empowering students and educators worldwide to explore the wonders of Earth's oceans and atmosphere through the lens of NASA's PACE mission. We strive to:

  • Increase ocean literacy and awareness of environmental challenges.
  • Provide engaging and accessible educational resources for all ages.
  • Inspire the next generation of scientists, explorers, and environmental stewards.
  • Promote collaboration and knowledge sharing among students, teachers, and researchers.
